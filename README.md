# ColorfulCalc
ColorfulCalc is a simple and powerful calculator great for fast and easy calculations. It is customizable with up to 18 different colors to choose from and has many features to boost your productivity.  Customizable Customize the look of your calculator with up to 18 different colors to choose from.   Productive When you start typing you are shown the numbers and operations you entered to help prevent calculation errors. There is even a backspace button for when you make a mistake and don't want to start all over. See your previous calculations with the history ability.
